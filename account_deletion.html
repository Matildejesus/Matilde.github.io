Delete Your Account

Swopsis — Account & Data Deletion Request

Summary: You can delete your account in-app or by email. Regular users are deleted immediately. Ambassadors are scheduled for deletion after a 30-day handover window so a new ambassador can be appointed.

Delete in the App (Recommended)

  1. Open the app → Profile.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Profile.
  3. (Optional) Choose a reason for deleting.
  4. If you are an Ambassador: email must be sent to delete account and change ambassador of group.
  5. Confirm the deletion request.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and authentication data
  • Your private messages/chats
  • Your wardrobe items and personal details

Timing

  • Regular users: deletion is processed immediately.
  • Ambassadors: account is queued for deletion; an admin has up to 30 days to appoint a new ambassador. After handover (or after 30 days), we complete the deletion.
